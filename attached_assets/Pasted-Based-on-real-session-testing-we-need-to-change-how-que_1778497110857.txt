The problem:

Waiting: P9+P10+P11+P12 (all lower skill)

Current behaviour: queued suggestions use P9-P12 together → two weak unbalanced games.

Court 1 next: P9 + P1 vs P10 + P3 (waiting player gets stronger partner)

The rule:
When building queued suggestions, always consider ALL players — waiting AND currently playing — and use the matchmaking algorithm to find the most balanced possible 4-player lineup for each court's next game.
The priority order for who plays next:

Players who have been waiting longest must be included — they are must-include
Fill remaining slots with players from the current court who have played the most consecutive games (rotating them off)
Players who just won their game get lower priority for the next round
Always optimise for skill balance across the two teams
